## GTM Trends

**Mux** made the biggest pricing move of the week, announcing their "biggest price drop in history"‚Äî20% reduction across encoding, storage, and delivery. What's smart about this announcement is how they framed it around infrastructure improvements rather than just competitive pressure.

Mux's approach to cold storage is clever too‚Äîthey eliminated the performance penalty and made it default for everyone. Now all assets, including static renditions, automatically move to cheaper storage tiers. This kind of automatic optimization removes a pricing decision from customers entirely.

The move to make static renditions a paid add-on for all quality levels is bold but makes sense. 90% of Mux customers don't use MP4s, so unbundling this feature lets them reduce core pricing for the majority while offering competitive pricing for those who need it.
